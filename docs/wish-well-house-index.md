# WISH WELL House Index

The WISH WELL system is the operating architecture for Lorraen Madre UFO.

It organizes life, business, documents, tasks, wishes, outputs, and strategy into a 12/13-house model.

The houses act like departments inside a Universal Family Office.

## Routing Rule

Every input should be classified into one primary house and, if needed, one secondary house.

Example:

- A custody agreement may belong to House 7, with House 4 as secondary.
- A business plan may belong to House 5, with House 2 as secondary.
- A trust document may belong to House 1 or House 9.
- A content campaign may belong to House 3 or House 10.

## Outcome

The WISH WELL house system lets the platform sort life, business, documents, strategy, and creative work without turning everything into one giant pile of “miscellaneous,” the most cursed folder name in human history.

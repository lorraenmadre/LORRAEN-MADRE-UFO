## Classification Rule

When the system receives an input, it can classify it by:

1. WISH WELL house
2. Planet or department
3. Destination tool
4. Next action

Examples:

- A public bio update may route to Sun and House 10.
- A document upload may route to Saturn, House 1, House 7, or New Castle.
- A marketing campaign may route to Mercury, Venus, House 3, or House 10.
- A funding pitch may route to Jupiter and House 2.
- A rabbit-hole idea may route to Neptune and Trello Dream Backlog.
